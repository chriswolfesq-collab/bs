How to play BS

  • All 52 cards are dealt out evenly. On your turn, play 1-4 face-down cards claiming they are all the required rank.
  • The required rank starts at Ace and advances (A, 2, 3, ... K, A, ...) after every turn, whether or not anyone challenges.
  • After a play, every other player gets the same window of time to call "BS" on the claim — it's a race, and it only takes one caller.
  • If someone calls BS: the cards are revealed. If the claim was false, the liar takes the whole pile. If it was true, the caller takes the whole pile.
  • If nobody calls BS before time runs out, the pile stays and play continues to the next player.
  • First player to get rid of all of their cards wins. That's it — no points.
